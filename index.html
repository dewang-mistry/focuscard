FocusCard A focus app for iOS

What it is

The index card you'd write a task on, as an iOS app.

You sit down to do one thing. Twenty minutes later you're three apps deep wondering why. FocusCard puts that one thing — huge on the screen, also on your Lock Screen — and nothing else. Because you work better when the thing you're trying to do is the only thing you can see.

Built for deep work, ADHD focus, and anyone who's ever propped a sticky note next to their laptop.

How it works

  • Pick one thing. Your iOS Reminders, grouped by list.
  • See it big. Tap a task. The screen turns black, the title fills it.
  • Keep it in sight. The screen stays awake. The task also lives on your Lock Screen and in the Dynamic Island.
  • Tap when done. Mark Complete checks it off in Reminders; tap anywhere else to pause.
